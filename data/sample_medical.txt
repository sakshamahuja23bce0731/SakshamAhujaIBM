MIMIC-III / MIMIC-IV (MIT)
A large, de-identified dataset of ICU patients' health data including vitals, lab tests, prescriptions, and clinical notes. Commonly used in mortality prediction, diagnosis classification, and treatment outcome analysis.

BioBERT / ClinicalBERT
Pretrained NLP models optimized for biomedical text, useful for named entity recognition, relation extraction, and question answering in clinical contexts.

PubMed Central Open Access (PMC-OA)
A vast collection of full-text biomedical articles. Ideal for training AI models in literature summarization, evidence extraction, and drug-disease relationship mining.
